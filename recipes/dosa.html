Dosa

Dosa, a staple of South Indian cuisine, is a thin, crispy pancake crafted from fermented rice and lentil batter, typically accompanied by coconut chutney and zesty sambar, delivering a delightful fusion of tangy, spicy, and savory notes that epitomize the essence of Indian culinary tradition.

Ingredients

  • Dosa batter
  • Butter
  • Raw banana filling
  • Coconut Chutney
  • Sambhar

Recipe

  1. Heat a non-stick pan and pour a ladle of dosa batter.
  2. Spread the batter in a circular motion to form a thin pancake.
  3. Drizzle butter around the edges and cook until golden brown.
  4. Place the raw banana filling in the center and fold the dosa.
  5. Serve hot with coconut chutney and sambhar.
