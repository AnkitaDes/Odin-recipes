PaniPuri

Pani puri, a popular Indian street food, is a delightful snack composed of hollow, crispy puris filled with a tangy and spicy mixture of flavored water, mashed potatoes, chickpeas, onions, and various chutneys. The burst of flavors and textures from the crispy shell, savory filling, and refreshing water makes it a favorite among food enthusiasts, offering a symphony of taste sensations in every bite. It's not just a snack; it's an experience that captures the essence of Indian culinary culture.

Ingredients

  • Puris
  • Boiled chickpeas
  • Boiled moong
  • Boiled brown chana
  • Mint-coriander chutney
  • Tamarind chutney
  • Sev
  • Chat masala

Recipe

  1. Make a hole in the puri and fill it with boiled chickpeas and moong.
  2. Add boiled brown chana to the puri.
  3. Fill the puri with mint-coriander chutney and tamarind chutney.
  4. Sprinkle sev and chat masala over the puri.
  5. Dip the puri in the flavored water and enjoy the burst of flavors!
