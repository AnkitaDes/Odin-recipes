Pizza

Pizza is a globally adored Italian classic, featuring a thin crust topped with tomato sauce, gooey cheese, and an array of delectable toppings, baked to golden perfection, promising a symphony of flavors in every bite.

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Yeast
  • Tomato sauce
  • Cheese
  • Bell peppers
  • Jalapinos
  • Coloured peppers
  • Olives
  • Pizza sauce
  • Italian seasoning

Recipe

  1. Roll out the pizza dough into a thin crust.
  2. Spread a layer of tomato sauce over the dough.
  3. Add Jain toppings like bell peppers, olives, corn, etc.
  4. Sprinkle grated Jain cheese over the toppings.
  5. Season with Italian seasoning and drizzle with olive oil.
  6. Slice and serve hot!
